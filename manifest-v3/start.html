Welcome to your trans-supportive Twitter extension.

New to the extension? Check the Soupcan website. Follow me on Twitter for the latest updates!

  1. Report transphobes for these reasons:
    • Intentional misgendering
    • Gore or transphobic comics or memes
    • Calling transgender a mental illness or delusion
    • Using transphobic slurs in a derogatory way
    • Comparing trans women to blackface
    • Self-declared 'gender-critical', 'terf' or transphobic
    And NOT these reasons alone:
    • Being mean to trans people
    • Disagreeing, e.g. with policies or being ignorant
    • Likes/retweets
    • One tweet alone is not enough, unless it's severe (multiple of the above in one tweet.) It could be a joke in poor taste, or context could be missing. Use the Soupcan > Search Tweets tool to investigate multiple tweets to identify a pattern.
  2. See more comprehensive reporting guidelines here.
  3. Do not attempt to hack or subvert the service or interfere with its operation for other users in any way.
  4. Use the appeal feature to unmark users who are not transphobic, or if you reported them accidentally.
